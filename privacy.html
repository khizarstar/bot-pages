🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Thank you for using our Discord bot. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and how we protect it.

📥 1. Information We Collect

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server (Guild) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Message IDs
  • Command usage and interaction data
  • Configuration settings created by server administrators
  • Moderation logs and audit data (if enabled)

We do not collect your Discord password, email address, payment information, or other sensitive personal information.

⚙️ 2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the bot's features and services.
  • Store server-specific settings.
  • Process commands and interactions.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and security.
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized use.
  • Troubleshoot technical issues.

💾 3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to operate the bot and provide its features. Some data may be removed automatically when it is no longer required.

🤝 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

Information may only be shared if:

  • Required by law.
  • Necessary to protect the security, rights, or integrity of the bot or its users.

🌐 5. Third-Party Services

Our bot operates through Discord and may use trusted third-party hosting or database providers. Your use of Discord is also subject to Discord's own policies.

🗑️ 6. Data Deletion

If the bot leaves a server, some server-specific data may be deleted automatically. Server administrators may also request deletion of their server's stored data where applicable.

🔐 7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to help protect stored information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

👶 8. Children's Privacy

Our bot is not intended for individuals who do not meet Discord's minimum age requirement. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

📝 9. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📞 10. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the bot's support server or the bot developer.

🛡️ By using this Discord bot, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.
