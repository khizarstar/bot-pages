COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

🛡️ Respect All Members

The cornerstone of our community is the mutual respect held between all members, regardless of background, opinion, or status. We enforce a strictly professional and welcoming environment where harassment, bullying, hate speech, or any form of targeted intimidation is met with immediate intervention.

Our moderation teams monitor these interactions to ensure that while heated discussions may occur, they never devolve into personal attacks. Maintaining a safe, inclusive space for every user is our absolute priority, and any behavior that compromises this safety threshold will be addressed to preserve our network’s integrity.

💬 No Spamming or Flooding

Operational clarity requires a clean chat interface. Spamming—defined as repeated messages, excessive emoji usage, symbol flooding, or unauthorized mass mentions—degrades the quality of communication and creates unnecessary noise across our server grid.

Automated filters are calibrated to detect and neutralize message velocity spikes in real-time. We expect all members to contribute to meaningful dialogues. Any attempt to circumvent these filters, whether through character manipulation or copy-paste flooding, will result in immediate disciplinary measures to keep our channels readable and productive.

🔗 Strict Anti-Advertising Policy

Our network infrastructure is curated to provide a focused experience for our members. Therefore, the unauthorized promotion of third-party servers, commercial services, social media platforms, or external invite links is strictly prohibited.

This policy extends across all public text channels, voice chat interactions, and direct messages sent between members. Promoting content without prior administrative authorization clutters our ecosystem and compromises the focus of our community. Any member found exploiting our platform for unauthorized marketing will face permanent restriction of access.

🧭 Proper Channel Utilization

Every channel within our network serves a distinct functional purpose. Structural integrity depends on members adhering to these specified topics. Posting off-topic content causes significant disorganization, making it difficult for administrative teams to track necessary data and support inquiries.

We urge members to carefully review channel descriptions and pinned messages before posting. By ensuring your contributions align with the channel's intent, you assist in maintaining an orderly, navigable, and high-efficiency community environment for everyone involved.

🚫 Zero Tolerance for Inappropriate Content

The maintenance of a safe environment is a non-negotiable operational standard. Content deemed NSFW, explicit, graphically violent, or inherently disturbing is strictly forbidden. This prohibition applies to all forms of media, including text snippets, embedded images, videos, and suggestive external links.

We utilize advanced moderation layers to ensure that all interactions remain appropriate for a general audience. The safety of our diverse member base relies on the total exclusion of this prohibited material, and any violations will be treated with the highest severity, leading to immediate and permanent removal from the network.
